Staff Notice: Payslip Collection & Pay Date Reminder

Hi Team,

As you are aware, we pay on the 5th of each calendar month, and wages are calculated from month to month. Please note, payment will never be later than the 5th, but I always do my best to process it earlier - usually by the 1st or 2nd, depending on public holidays and other factors.

Once your wages have been received in your account, please come and see me to collect your wage slips.

Thank you for your cooperation!
